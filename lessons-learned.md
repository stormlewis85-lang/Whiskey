# Whiskey Pedia — Lessons Learned

Patterns, surprises, and failure modes captured from development sessions.
Reviewed during weekly Sunday cadence and promoted to framework-level rules
when a pattern appears across 3+ projects.

---

## Format

Each entry follows this structure:
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Context:** What was being attempted
- **Surprise/Failure:** What went wrong or was unexpected
- **Root Cause:** Why it happened
- **Resolution:** What fixed it
- **Rule:** The generalizable principle (if any)
